For Artists

Your Music. Your Terms.

What Artists Get on P.A.R.

💰

Keep every dollar. Payments go straight to your wallet via Solana — instantly, not quarterly.

🌐

IPFS Storage You Control

Your tracks live on a decentralized network. No corporate server can delist your music overnight.

Community Discovery

Fans ping your tracks to friends. Music rises because real people share it — not because you bought playlist placement.

On-Chain Provenance

Every upload is recorded on Solana. Transparent, immutable proof of when and how your music entered the network.

No Minimum Thresholds

No 250-stream minimum before you see a cent. Every stream counts from day one.

Token Rewards (Coming)

Early artists will help shape a custom Solana token that rewards creation and community participation.
