For Music Fans

Discover Music Through
People, Not Algorithms

Tired of the same playlists pushed by corporate algorithms? On P.A.R., your friends and community curate what you hear. Ping a song. Share a signal. Find music that actually matters.

What Fans Get on P.A.R.

⚡

Ping with a Song

Send a track directly to a friend. Real recommendations from real people — not a black-box algorithm.

📻

Most Pinged Radio

Tune into what's trending in the P.A.R. community right now — a live playlist of the most-shared tracks.

🎧

Always Free to Listen

No paywall, no ads tier, no premium upsell. Stream freely and support artists directly when you want to.

💚

Support Artists Directly

When you tip or pay for a stream, 100% goes to the artist. P.A.R. takes zero cut from your support.

🌍

Censorship-Resistant Catalog

Music lives on IPFS. As long as the community pins a track, it stays available — no corporate takedown.

🪙

Earn for Participating

Share, seed, and curate — early fans will earn token rewards for helping grow the network.
