Post Apocalyptic Radio

(P.A.R.)

Your Decentralized, Social Music Frontier

Imagine a world where music isn't controlled by a few central platforms, but by the artists and communities who love it. That's the vision behind Post Apocalyptic Radio (P.A.R.) — a new kind of music streaming experience built for the decentralized future.

What Makes P.A.R. Unique?

🎵

Artist-Owned Music, Powered by IPFS

Artists upload directly to IPFS — a global, decentralized network where files persist across many computers, making music more resilient and censorship-resistant.

🛡️

Solana Blockchain for Trust

Song metadata — artist, title, IPFS link — is immutably recorded on Solana. Every upload is transparent and verifiable by anyone.

⚡

Social Discovery: Ping with a Song

Forget black-box algorithms. Discover music through friends and community. Send a song recommendation directly to another user with a ping.

📻

Most Pinged Radio

Tune into what's hot in the P.A.R. universe. A live playlist of the songs pinged most by the community — real discovery, not algorithmic manipulation.

🪙

Custom Solana Token Incentives

The vision includes a custom Solana token to reward artists and incentivize listeners who share, participate, and help seed music on the IPFS network.

💚

0% Platform Cut

Every payment routes directly to the artist via Solana smart contracts. No intermediary, no quarterly wait, no 30% cut.
