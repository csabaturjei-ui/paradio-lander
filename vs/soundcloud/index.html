What PAR Does That SoundCloud Can't

The Go+ model and corporate investors create structural ceilings SoundCloud can't remove

🌐

Decentralized Storage

SoundCloud is one bankruptcy, acquisition, or policy shift away from your catalog disappearing. It nearly happened in 2017 — the company avoided collapse by a thin margin. PAR stores tracks on IPFS: a distributed network the community pins and maintains. No single point of failure, no single point of control.

⛓️

Zero Middlemen

SoundCloud's Repost Select program takes 15% off every stream before paying you monthly. PAR's Solana smart contracts route micropayments directly from listener to artist wallet — on every play, instantly, with no intermediary taking a cut and no minimum balance before you can withdraw.

📡

Community Curation

SoundCloud's recommended tracks surface based on follows, reposts, and a recommendation engine shaped by promoted content. On PAR, the playlist is curated by listeners who pin tracks they genuinely believe in — no ad budget needed to surface alongside artists people love.

Music Shouldn't Live Behind a Paywall

P.A.R. is in closed beta. Artists and listeners who join now shape the protocol — no Go+ tier, no minimum streams, no corporate board deciding who gets heard.
