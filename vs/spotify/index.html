What PAR Does That Spotify Never Will

Three pillars Spotify's business model structurally prevents

🌐

Decentralized Storage

Tracks on PAR live on IPFS — a distributed network with no single server that can be switched off. Spotify can delist your music overnight with no appeal. On PAR, the community pins tracks and they persist as long as anyone cares about them. No host, no hostage.

⛓️

Zero Middlemen

Spotify sits between you and your fans, extracting 30% before your distributor and label take their share — artists often net under 15%. PAR runs on Solana smart contracts: when a listener streams your track, payment routes directly to your wallet. No intermediary. No delay. No cut.

📡

Community Curation

Spotify's algorithm is engineered to serve label relationships and ad revenue — not discovery. On PAR, listeners curate by pinning tracks they actually believe in. Music rises because real people put their stake behind it, not because a playlist-pitching campaign bought a spot.
